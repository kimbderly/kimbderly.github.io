Remember to take your
pills!
In collaboration with 3 others, the Smart Pill Box relies on 2 MSP432 Microcontrollers to remind users to take their medication at fixed intervals. A buzzer was used to remind the user while light and weight sensors were used to detect if the user had taken the medication. A Wi-Fi component was implemented to send an email to caregivers if the user forgets to eat their medication.
I²C Communication was implemented to trigger the weight reading of the pill box.
