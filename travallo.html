Social Media Infused Travel Planner
In collaboration with 4 others, Travallo was designed as a social media travel planner catered to users' personalities. A personality test will determine one's travel personality which aids in recommending activities to the user. Users may upload and like photos, and follow other users. An offline component allows users to save their planners as an ICS file to add into their calendars.
As part of a HCI module, this web app focuses on the following design principles: Norman’s Usability Principles, Gestalt Principles and Nielsen’s Usability Heuristics.
